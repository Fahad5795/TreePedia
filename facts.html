Trees play an important role in the water cycle, grounding the water in their roots and releasing it into the atmosphere. Here are some interesting facts about trees and the impact of deforestation around the world.

Earth

The Earth is what we all have in common.

Earth is about 57,308,738 square miles, of which about 33% is desert and about 24% is mountainous. Total land area leaves 15.77 billion acres of habitable land. This equates to 7% of the Earth's surface covered by trees which is 3 Trillion Trees.
